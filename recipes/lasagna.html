Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna may take some time, but the reults are totally worth the time and effort required.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
